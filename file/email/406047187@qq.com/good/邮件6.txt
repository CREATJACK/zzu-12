Create automated GUI tests for your embedded app
Learn how to design and execute automated, functional tests for the GUI frontend of your embedded HMI with The Qt Company's Squish GUI Tester:
- cross-compile Squish for use on a target
- automate your Application Under Test
- record, replay, and refactor test scripts in a modern scripting language
- remotely control your device from anywhere
- create test reports from your results
We'll also introduce Qt's Test Center: a test result management and analysis platform that aggregates and statistically treats your test result data for you.
Sign up and join us on June 28 and start creating robust, automated GUI tests!
